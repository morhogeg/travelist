# Privacy Policy

**Travelist**  
**Effective Date:** December 14, 2025

---

## Overview

Travelist is a travel recommendation app that helps you save and organize places you want to visit. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

---

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally (On Your Device)
The following data is stored **only on your device** and never leaves your phone unless you opt into cloud sync:

- Your saved places and recommendations
- Routes and trip itineraries
- Collections you create
- App preferences (theme, filters)

### Data Collected for Cloud Sync (Optional)
If you choose to create an account for cloud backup:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Email address | Account login and identification |
| Your saved places | Sync across devices |

### Data Processed by AI (Optional)
When you use the "Add from text" feature:
- Text you enter is sent to OpenRouter AI to extract place names
- This text is processed but **not stored** by the AI service
- We do not send any personal information to the AI

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contacts
- ❌ Photos (unless you add them to places)
- ❌ Analytics or usage tracking
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Device fingerprinting

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Delete all data** by uninstalling the app
- **Delete cloud data** by signing out and requesting deletion
- **Use the app without an account** (all data stays on device)

---

## Data Security

- All cloud communications use HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are never stored in plain text
- Local data is stored in your device's secure storage

---

## Children's Privacy

Travelist is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
