## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a place?
Tap the **+** button on the home screen. You can either fill in the details manually or paste text and let AI extract the place information.

### Where is my data stored?
All your data is stored locally on your device by default. If you create an account, you can optionally sync to the cloud.

### Can I use the app without an account?
Yes! The app works fully offline without an account. Accounts are only needed for cloud backup and syncing across devices.

### How do I delete my data?
- **Local data:** Uninstall the app
- **Cloud data:** Go to Settings â†’ Sign Out, then contact us to request deletion

### How do I create a route?
Go to the **Routes** tab and tap **Create Route**. You can then add places to your route from your saved recommendations.
